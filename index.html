  • Introduction

    Subheadline

    Since 1993 I've produced work ranging from dynamic web applications and interactive multimedia features, to marketing collateral. I can hand-code with the best of them but I thrive in a collaborative role as the bridge between developers and stakeholders. In that capacity, my big-picture understanding of both delivery mechanisms and business goals informs design decisions. Please read on to learn about what I can do for you.

  • Capabilities

    Subheadline

    • Full life cycle design from strategy through development
    • Wireframes and interaction flows, UI/UX design, prototyping, visual design, layout, creation of assets, testing, documentation
    • Front-end development within an Agile environment, integrating code with databases and software, collaboration with developers
    • Project management, style guides, scoping, vendor relations, de-coding mixed messages, focusing on goals
    • Art direction, logo design, graphic design through delivery of printed materials
    • Content creation, multimedia features, photo shoots, copy writing, editing, multi-channel communications planning

  • Deliverables

    Subheadline

    Web application UI, websites, multimedia galleries, interactive presentations, icons, animations, style guides, logos, landing pages, email newsletters, collateral, publications, signage, annual reports, nocino
